Welcome to the world of Onikishi, an adventure game where you take on the role of a powerful and feared Demon Knight tasked with protecting your realm from the heroes who seek to exploit it. Will you rise to the challenge and emerge victorious, or will the heroes overcome you and bring your world crashing down around you? Gather your forces, sharpen your skills, and get ready to face off against the mightiest heroes the world has ever known. The fate of your realm is in your hands - will you be the hero your people need, or will you succumb to the forces of darkness and fall into oblivion? The choice is yours in Onikishi.
